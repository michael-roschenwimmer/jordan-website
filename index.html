About

I've been involved with music for over two decades and I love sharing my talents with the community. Whether it's by teaching lessons, providing music at a private event, or entertaining people while they're enjoying a night out, nothing brings me more joy than brightening up someone's day with music.

I am available for hire to teach guitar or play gigs (weddings, restaurants, resorts, events, etc.) I play primarily covers, have over 10 years performing experience, and have a wide repertoire containing hits from the 70s to today.

Lessons

I am available to teach guitar lessons to students of all ages and skill levels. My specialties are finger picking, acoustic/folk styles and playing by ear, but can teach any other style up to at least an intermediate level. I would love to help you learn your favorite songs, or improve your playing!
